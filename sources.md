# Idea Sources

## Free

| Source | URL | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Fix My Itch | https://razorpay.com/m/fix-my-itch | 15,000+ India-specific problems, scored by severity + market size |
| YC Requests for Startups | https://www.ycombinator.com/rfs | ~15 high-conviction problem themes from YC's investment thesis |
| Indie Hackers Ideas | https://www.indiehackers.com/ideas | Proven ideas with real MRR — great for validating a space |
| Find Startup Ideas | https://www.findstartupideas.com | Search Hacker News pain points by topic (15 searches/day) |
| Needgap | https://needgap.com | Community-submitted problems, upvoted by real users |
| OpinionX 99 Problems | https://www.opinionx.co/99-problems | Founding problems behind 99 successful companies — good for frameworks |

## Paid / Freemium

| Source | URL | Cost | What you get |
|---|---|---|---|
| BigIdeasDB | https://bigideasdb.com | Freemium | AI-aggregated pain points from Reddit, G2, Upwork, App stores |
| StartupIdeasDB | https://startupideasdb.com | Paid | 12,000+ ideas from 10+ platforms incl. Flipkart/Amazon India |
| IdeaPicker | https://ideapicker.io | One-time | 25,000+ Reddit-sourced ideas with market analysis + revenue forecast |
| PainOnSocial | https://painonsocial.com | $19–49/mo | Scan any subreddit for ranked pain points with Reddit source links |

| GapHunt | https://www.producthunt.com/products/gaphunt | Unknown | App Store / Play Store gaps mined from 1–2 star reviews |
| Starter Story Problems | https://www.starterstory.com/data/problems | ~$99/yr | 91 problems each with a proven founder case study + revenue data |
