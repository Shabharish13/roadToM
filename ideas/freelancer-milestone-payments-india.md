# Freelancer Milestone Payment + Accountability (India-first)

**Date:** 2026-03-28
**Source:** Razorpay Fix My Itch (Itch Score: 76) + Hacker News + X/Twitter
**Status:** Validated

---

## Problem Statement

- **Who:** Indian freelancers (designers, developers, writers, marketers) doing domestic client work
- **What:** Clients ghost after partial payments, dispute completed work, or simply refuse to pay — with no lightweight enforcement mechanism short of civil court
- **When:** After work is completed or mid-project, when client no longer needs the freelancer's cooperation

**Workarounds people use today:**
- Requiring 50% upfront (loses clients, leaves the rest unprotected)
- Filing in Small Claims Court for ≤₹5L (slow, intimidating, rarely used)
- Publicly naming clients on social media (risky, unscalable)
- Using Freelancer.com or Fiverr (only works for new client relationships, not existing ones)
